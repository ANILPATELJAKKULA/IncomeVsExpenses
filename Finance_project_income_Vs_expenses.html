Backstory

* Many individuals struggle with maintaining a healthy balance between their income and expenses, often leading to poor savings habits and financial stress. This project aims to analyze individual financial data to understand spending patterns, savings behavior, and the overall financial health of an individual.

* The project compares monthly income with various categories of expenses (such as investments, emi loans, transportation, etc.) to identify trends, gaps, and opportunities for better financial planning. The goal is to provide actionable insights to help individuals track their finances, optimize spending, and increase savings.

Business Objectives

  • Analyze how individuals across different income levels manage their expenses.
  • Identify patterns of overspending or saving across age groups and genders.
  • Identify individuals at risk of financial distress (e.g., high EMI, low income).
  • Compare home loan EMI against annual income to determine affordability.
